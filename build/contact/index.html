Account Troubleshooting Guide

Experiencing issues with your 92 Jeeto account? Before contacting support, try these common solutions that resolve the majority of account-related problems our players encounter. This self-service guide covers everything from login failures to balance discrepancies and can save you valuable time.

Cannot Log In

  • →Double-check that you are entering the correct phone number and password — pay attention to country code and spacing.
  • →Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password via OTP. Ensure your phone can receive SMS messages.
  • →Clear the app cache by going to your device settings, then Apps, then 92 Jeeto, then Storage, and tap "Clear Cache."
  • →Update to the latest version of the 92 Jeeto app from our download page.
  • →If your account appears locked or suspended, contact support immediately with your registered phone number.

OTP Not Received

  • →Wait at least 60 seconds before requesting a new OTP — messages can be delayed during peak hours.
  • →Ensure your phone has an active SIM card with network coverage and that your inbox is not full.
  • →Check if your phone or carrier has a spam filter that might be blocking messages from shortcodes.
  • →Restart your phone and try again. If the problem continues, contact our support team for manual verification.

Balance Discrepancy

  • →Pull down to refresh your wallet screen — balance updates may take a few seconds to reflect after a game or transaction.
  • →Review your transaction history to verify all recent deposits, withdrawals, bets, and winnings.
  • →Note that bonus funds and real funds may be displayed separately — check both balances.
  • →If you still notice a discrepancy, take a screenshot and contact support with the exact amount difference and your recent transaction history.

Payment Support Guide

92 Jeeto partners with Pakistan's most trusted payment providers to deliver seamless transactions. However, occasional issues can arise due to network delays, carrier maintenance, or input errors. Below is a comprehensive guide to resolving the most common payment issues for each supported method.

JazzCash Issues

Deposit not credited: Verify the transaction was successful in your JazzCash app history. If the amount was deducted but not reflected in your 92 Jeeto wallet within 15 minutes, send a screenshot of the JazzCash confirmation to our support team.

Withdrawal pending: JazzCash withdrawals usually complete in under 10 minutes. Ensure your JazzCash account is active and the registered number matches your withdrawal request. During Jazz system maintenance windows, withdrawals may take slightly longer.

Transaction failed: Check that your JazzCash account has sufficient balance (for deposits) and that your mobile account limits have not been exceeded. Retry after a few minutes, and if the issue persists, contact Jazz customer service alongside our team for coordinated resolution.

Easypaisa Issues

Deposit not credited: Check your Easypaisa transaction history to confirm the payment was processed. If the money left your account but is not showing in your 92 Jeeto wallet, wait 15 minutes and then contact support with the Easypaisa transaction reference number.

Withdrawal pending: Easypaisa withdrawals are typically processed in under 10 minutes. Ensure your Easypaisa account is active and not under any restrictions. If the withdrawal status shows "processing" for over 30 minutes, contact our support team immediately.

Account mismatch: Your Easypaisa number must match the number registered on your 92 Jeeto account. If there is a mismatch, update your payment details in the app settings or contact support for assistance with linking the correct number.

Bank Transfer & USDT Support

Bank transfers: Standard bank transfers may take 1-24 hours to process depending on your bank and whether the transfer is made during business hours. Inter-bank transfers (IBFT) are usually faster. Always double-check the account details before confirming a transfer. If a bank deposit is not reflected after 24 hours, send the bank receipt or transaction reference to our support team.

USDT transactions: USDT deposits and withdrawals depend on blockchain network confirmation times. Ensure you are sending to the correct wallet address and using the correct network (TRC-20 or ERC-20 as specified in the app). Network congestion can occasionally cause delays. Never send tokens on an unsupported network, as this may result in permanent loss of funds.

Responsible Gaming

At 92 Jeeto, we are deeply committed to promoting responsible gaming among all our players. Gaming should always be a form of entertainment, not a source of financial stress. We provide tools and resources to help you maintain control over your gaming activity and encourage you to seek help if you ever feel that gaming is becoming a problem.

Tools We Provide

  • •Deposit limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits to control your spending and stay within a budget that is comfortable for you.
  • •Session reminders: Enable notifications that remind you how long you have been playing, helping you take regular breaks.
  • •Self-exclusion: If you need a break, you can request temporary or permanent self-exclusion from the platform by contacting our support team.
  • •Activity history: Review your complete gaming and transaction history at any time to understand your playing patterns.

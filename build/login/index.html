Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an essential extra barrier between attackers and your 92 Jeeto account. Even if someone manages to obtain your password through a data breach on another service or a phishing attempt, they still cannot access your account without the second verification factor. We strongly recommend that every player enables 2FA for maximum protection.

How 2FA Works on 92 Jeeto

When you enable two-factor authentication, every login attempt requires two separate pieces of verification: your password (something you know) and a one-time code sent to your phone (something you have). This dual requirement means that compromising a single factor is not enough to breach your account. The verification code changes with every login attempt, so it cannot be reused or predicted.

To enable 2FA, log into your 92 Jeeto account, navigate to Settings, then Security, and toggle on "Two-Factor Authentication." You will be asked to verify your phone number one final time to activate the feature. From that point forward, every login will require both your password and a fresh OTP code.

Why 2FA Matters

  • •Protects against password theft — even leaked passwords are useless without your phone
  • •Prevents unauthorized withdrawals — sensitive financial actions require the second factor
  • •Alerts you to breach attempts — if you receive an unexpected OTP, you know someone tried to log in
  • •Industry best practice — banks, email providers, and major platforms all recommend 2FA
  • •Gives you complete control — only the person holding your registered phone can approve access
